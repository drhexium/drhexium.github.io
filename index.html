Who We Are

Adveos was founded in 2015 offering custom ASIC solutions for RF/mm-wave systems and sensor applications. The company rapidly acquired an international customer base and managed to establish a significant presence across multiple markets and sectors.

In 2018, we started developing IP solutions mainly related to wireless connectivity. For the years to follow, Adveos executed a hybrid business model in both ASIC design services and IP core development.

In December 2020, Adveos was acquired by Beken Corporation, a leading player in wireless communication solutions. As an integral part of the new organization, we are now working in delivering complete wireless communication platforms.

Our expertise

Analog IC design

RF and mm-wave high performance transceiver design for a variety of applications, such as Wi-Fi , LTE, Bluetooth, satellite communications and others. Experience in design nodes ranging from BiCMOS technologies down to 7nm CMOS.

Digital IC design

Digital IC design for multi-million gate System-On-Chip (SoC) realizations using state-of-the-art architectures and low-power design techniques. Full SoC verification and pre-silicon validation through FPGA prototyping

System engineering

End-to-end baseband algorithm development, including calibration, initialization and radio resource control tasks. System characterization, compliance testing and validation. Advanced verification through automated test-fixtures

Embedded firmware

Embedded software development for real-time applications on resource-constrained SoC devices, accommodating cutting-edge telecommunications software and wireless protocol stacks

Technology

Research on wireless communications systems. System architecture, software modelling and product requirements management. Participation in standardization and dissemination activities

Bluetooth Low Energy locator

ADV361 uses BluetoothÂ® Low Energy Direction Finding technology in Angle-of-Arrival (AoA) and Angle-of-Departure (AoD) modes. It uses best-in-class algorithms to support stand-alone dual-axis angle detection.

Integration with an appropriate Real Time Location System (RTLS) enables superior 3D positioning accuracy (better than 30 cm).

A smart embedded firmware implementation allows customization to achieve the performance targets of different applications (e.g., positioning accuracy, detection speed, number of supported tags).

By offering a unique combination of properties, ADV361 is the perfect solution for a wide range of indoor positioning applications and markets.

Innovative Digital Transmitter of Integrated Systems â HIDIT

ADVEOS participates in HIDIT, a research program on innovative digital transmitter architectures. The project includes the implementation in 28 nm CMOS technology, of an innovative fully digital transmitter (all-digital transmitter) for low-energy wireless protocols such as Bluetooth LE, Zigbee. The proposed transmitter is characterized by particularly low energy consumption, low complexity, easy adaptation to each application, and flexibility in use. The fully digital architecture exhibits reduced static consumption, low development cost as digital circuits require less silicon surface and generally less design time, offers great parameterization capability of the system, inherent linearity in the transmitted signal, and finally compatibility with multiple low-energy consumption protocols.

Participating entities: ADVEOS, ELLAB, VLSILAB
