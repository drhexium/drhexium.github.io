Who We Are

Adveos was founded in 2015 offering custom ASIC solutions for RF/mm-wave systems and sensor applications. The company rapidly acquired an international customer base and managed to establish a significant presence across multiple markets and sectors.

In 2018, we started developing IP solutions mainly related to wireless connectivity. For the years to follow, Adveos executed a hybrid business model in both ASIC design services and IP core development.

In December 2020, Adveos was acquired by Beken Corporation, a leading player in wireless communication solutions. As an integral part of the new organization, we are now working in delivering complete wireless communication platforms.

Test Engineer

ADVEOS Microelectronic Systems PC develops and commercially exploits solutions in the field of wireless communications, with a major emphasis on WiFi and Bluetooth products. The company has an international customer base and worldwide presence with its main R&D groups located in Greece, Armenia and China. As of December 2020, Adveos is member of the multinational organisation Beken Corporation. There is a unique opportunity for a Test Engineer to join our highly skilled and motivated company, within the System Team.

Responsibilities
  • Gather and report data from test equipment (spectrum analyzers, vector signal generators, oscilloscopes, power supplies, etc.) in either an automated or a manual way.
  • Perform testing and prepare the relevant documentation for products being evaluated in the Lab. These products will be – but not limited to - WiFi and Bluetooth modules.
  • Post-process the test results in an efficient and detailed way, through advanced spreadsheet development and scripting.
  • Understand and potentially participate in the design of test fixtures for RF and system characterization, verification and validation.
  • Organize and setup lab procedures, such as, lab inventory, new test instrumentation needs, internal Wiki maintenance etc.
  • Work closely with other teams besides the System team, such as the Digital and analog teams, to understand test requirements and execute test characterisation plans.
  • Build/modify/create test scripts for in-house applications including testing for data extraction, data gathering, and performance.
Main Requirements
  • University degree in Engineering, Physics or other related discipline with strong emphasis on electronics
  • Great attention to detail and work quality
  • Programming languages and Software knowledge – Python, Matlab, C/C++
  • Proficient spreadsheet usage
  • Understand embedded software
  • Test automation
  • Good understanding of RF/Analog principles
  • Good communication skills
  • Excellent use of English language
Would be a Plus
  • Familiarity with 802.11b/g/n/ac/ax and Bluetooth standards
  • Familiarity with evaluating ASICs and FPGA programming
  • MCU programming
  • Experience in RF / Analog design and simulation
  • Experience in collaborating with remote sites
Work environment

You will be based at our office & lab location in Neo Psychiko, Athens. Our environment is designed to encourage collaborative working across all teams. You will be joining the System Team, and be within close reach of a specialist in various disciplines, including design, modelling and simulation, and electronics hardware.

December 22, 2020 - ADVEOS acquired by Beken Corporation

Beken Corporation (SSE: 603068), a key player in wireless communication solutions announced today that it has it has completed the acquisition of Adveos Microelectronic Systems, an RF and mixed-signal IP provider based in Greece.

The acquisition will strengthen Beken’s wireless communication capabilities and will enhance the company's growth momentum in a time where the rapid developments in wireless protocols provide significant opportunities both for the domestic and foreign markets. Furthermore, this transaction clearly demonstrates Beken’s strategy to be gradually promoted into a world-class wireless solutions provider company.

  • The acquisition will strengthen Beken’s wireless communication capabilities
  • Beken has been focusing on the design and development of wireless communication
  • In Adveos we have found a great team that perfectly complements our vision

Mr. Pengfei Zhang, Beken’s Chairman, said: "Beken has been focusing on the design and development of wireless communication ICs for a long time by providing complete wireless communication solutions for various application scenarios such as wireless connectivity, intelligent transportation and the Internet of Things. The acquisition of Adveos will enable Beken to strengthen its strategic position in these markets, bringing new growth opportunities in terms of revenue and products, as well as enhancing the company's market competitiveness and long-term profitability. In Adveos we have found a great team that perfectly complements our vision to become the leading wireless chipset supplier. We aim to further expanding the team in Athens and increase substantially its footprint over the coming years.”

“We are delighted to become part of a leading organization such as Beken and look forward to working together in the development of the next generation wireless platforms” said George Dimitropoulos, CEO and Co-Founder of Adveos. “It is an exciting opportunity for Adveos, within the Beken family, to be a key member in the design of next generation wireless products that will help improve the lives of millions”.
