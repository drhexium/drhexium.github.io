Who We Are

Adveos was founded in 2015 offering custom ASIC solutions for RF/mmWave systems and Sensor applications. The company acquired rapidly an international customer base and managed to establish a significant presence across multiple markets and sectors. In 2018, we started developing our own IP solutions mainly related to wireless connectivity. For the years to follow, Adveos executed a hybrid business model of both ASIC design service and IP core development. In December 2020, Adveos was acquired by Beken Corporation, a leading player in wireless communication solutions. As an integral part of the new organization, Adveos is now working in delivering complete wireless communication platforms.
